What is Bible

by DAVID ROY

Introduction

The Bible is a collection of sacred texts or scriptures held to be authoritative in Christianity. It contains history, poetry, prophecy, and spiritual guidance from God to humanity.

Chat Bible Verse

John 3:16: For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life.

Summary of the Bible

The Bible is divided into two main sections: the Old Testament and the New Testament. The Old Testament lays the foundation of God's covenant with humanity. The New Testament introduces Jesus Christ and the new covenant through His death and resurrection.

Story Analysis

From the creation of the world in Genesis, to the parting of the Red Sea in Exodus, to the teachings of Jesus in the Gospels, the Bible presents the redemptive plan of God. Each story contributes to a larger picture of grace and truth.
